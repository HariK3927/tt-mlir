graph tests
- no sharding annotations

- full sdy annotations for arguments and no op sdy annotations
- partial sdy annotations for arguments and no op sdy annotations
- full op sdy annotations and no sdy argument annotations
- partial op sdy annotations and no sdy argument annotations
- full argument and full op annotations
- partial argument and full op annotations
- full argument and partial op annotations
- multiple functions within module
- moduleOp with constants

negative tests
- gspmd graph
- sdy annotations but no meshOp
- sdy annotations and >1 meshOps
- no annotations and invalid user mesh shape
- argument tensor dimension not disible by sdy annotation
- op tensor dimension not disible by sdy annotation
